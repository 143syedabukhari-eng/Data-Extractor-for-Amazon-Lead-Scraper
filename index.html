Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Data Extractor for Amazon & Lead Scraper ("the Extension") handles information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a secure experience while using our tool.

Zero-Data Collection Policy: We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data, browsing history, or the data you extract to any external servers.

1. Information We Access

The Extension requires specific permissions to function correctly on Amazon marketplaces:

  • Active Tab & Host Permissions: Used solely to read product data (titles, prices, ratings, sales volume) from Amazon domains while you are actively using the tool.
  • Storage: Used to save your "Starred" products and extension settings locally on your computer. This data never leaves your device.
  • Downloads: Used exclusively to save the files you export (CSV, Excel, JSON) to your local "Downloads" folder.

2. Local Data Processing

All data extraction and processing occur entirely within your local browser environment. The data you scrape from Amazon is held in your browser's temporary memory or local storage and is only transferred when you choose to download it to your physical hard drive.

3. No Third-Party Sharing

Because we do not collect your data, we cannot and do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking cookies.

4. External Links

The Extension provides links to Amazon product pages. Please be aware that once you click these links and navigate to Amazon, their own privacy policy and terms of service apply.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through the feedback section within the extension or via our support channels.
